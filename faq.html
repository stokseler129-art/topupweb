❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I top up? ▼
Simply choose your game, select the product, enter your game ID, and complete the payment. No login is required.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
No, our system is designed for quick access. You can make purchases using only your game ID.
How long does the process take? ▼
Most transactions are processed instantly or within a few minutes after payment confirmation.
What payment methods are available? ▼
We support various payment methods such as bank transfer and e-wallets, depending on availability.
Is this platform safe? ▼
Yes. We prioritize security and transparency. Your data is not stored unnecessarily and all transactions are handled securely.
Can I track my order? ▼
Yes, you can check your transaction status using your transaction ID on the "Cek Transaksi" page.
What if my top-up fails? ▼
If your transaction fails, please contact our support team via WhatsApp for assistance.
